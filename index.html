🜃 Earth Element

Bhramari Pranayama

Humming Bee Breath

Benefits

  • Calms the mind and reduces stress
  • Relieves tension and anxiety
  • Improves concentration and memory
  • Reduces blood pressure
  • Grounds and centers the body

Instructions

  1. Sit in a comfortable position with spine straight
  2. Close your eyes and relax your body
  3. Place your index fingers on your ears (Shanmukhi Mudra)
  4. Inhale deeply through your nose
  5. Exhale slowly while making a humming sound like a bee
  6. Feel the vibrations in your head

Duration

Inhale: 4 seconds  |  Exhale (Hum): 6 seconds

Recommended: 5-10 rounds

🜄 Water Element

Sheetali Pranayama

Cooling Breath

Benefits

  • Cools the body and mind
  • Reduces body heat and fever
  • Calms anger and anxiety
  • Purifies the blood
  • Improves digestion

Instructions

  1. Sit comfortably with spine erect
  2. Roll your tongue into a tube shape
  3. Extend tongue slightly outside the mouth
  4. Inhale slowly through the rolled tongue
  5. Close your mouth and hold the breath
  6. Exhale slowly through your nose

Duration

Inhale: 5 seconds  |  Hold: 3 seconds  |  Exhale: 5 seconds

Recommended: 8-12 rounds

🜂 Fire Element

Kapalbhati Pranayama

Skull Shining Breath

Benefits

  • Energizes the body and mind
  • Improves lung capacity
  • Strengthens abdominal muscles
  • Detoxifies the body
  • Increases metabolic rate

Instructions

  1. Sit in a comfortable meditative pose
  2. Take a deep breath in
  3. Exhale forcefully through the nose
  4. Focus on forceful exhalation, passive inhalation
  5. Contract abdominal muscles during exhalation
  6. Perform rapid succession of breaths

Duration

Rapid breaths: 30 breaths per round

Recommended: 3-5 rounds with rest in between

🜁 Air Element

Nadi Shodhana

Alternate Nostril Breathing

Benefits

  • Balances left and right hemispheres of brain
  • Calms the nervous system
  • Reduces stress and anxiety
  • Improves focus and concentration
  • Purifies nadis (energy channels)

Instructions

  1. Sit comfortably with spine straight
  2. Use right thumb to close right nostril
  3. Inhale slowly through left nostril
  4. Close left nostril with ring finger
  5. Exhale through right nostril
  6. Inhale through right, exhale through left

Duration

Inhale: 4 seconds  |  Hold: 4 seconds  |  Exhale: 4 seconds

Recommended: 10-15 rounds

🜀 Ether Element

Ujjayi Pranayama

Victorious Ocean Breath

Benefits

  • Enhances mental clarity and focus
  • Increases oxygen intake
  • Regulates body temperature
  • Relieves insomnia
  • Connects with higher consciousness

Instructions

  1. Sit in a comfortable meditation posture
  2. Slightly constrict the back of your throat
  3. Breathe through your nose with mouth closed
  4. Create an ocean-like sound in your throat
  5. Inhale deeply and slowly
  6. Exhale with the same constricted throat

Duration

Inhale: 5 seconds  |  Hold: 2 seconds  |  Exhale: 5 seconds

Recommended: 10-20 rounds
